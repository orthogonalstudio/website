orthogonal studio is an Izmir-based independent game studio, which aim to develop a deep technical expertise on driving, racing, and navigation mechanics for the PC platform. In order to minimize the operational burden and repetitive work in the development process, we are building our own proprietary framework, "The Frame." Thanks to this purpose-driven infrastructure that is free from unnecessary complexity, we aim to focus all our energy on flawlessly working control mechanisms and innovative game concepts. Please, feel free to reach us for anything; you always be welcomed warmly.
